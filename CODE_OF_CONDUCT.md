# Code of Conduct

loom is a technical project with a small, focused community. We keep this simple.

## The short version

Be direct, be honest, be respectful. Debate ideas, not people. No harassment, discrimination, or bad faith.

## What we value

- **Technical rigour** — argue from first principles, cite evidence, be willing to be wrong
- **Directness** — say what you mean, disagree openly, skip the politics
- **Good faith** — assume others are trying to build something good
- **Simplicity** — the best contribution is often removing complexity, not adding it

## What we don't tolerate

- Harassment or personal attacks
- Discrimination of any kind
- Bad faith contributions (spam, sabotage, plagiarism)
- Dismissing contributions without explanation

## Enforcement

Issues or PRs that violate this will be closed. Repeated violations mean removal from the project.

If something feels wrong, open an issue or reach out directly.
