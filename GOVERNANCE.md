# Governance

loom is currently a solo project maintained by [@lorenzh](https://github.com/lorenzh).

## Decision making

Decisions are made by the maintainer. For significant architectural changes, an ADR is written first and open for comment for at least 72 hours before being accepted.

If you disagree with a decision, open an issue and argue your case. Good arguments backed by evidence get heard.

## Roadmap

The roadmap lives in the README status checklist and open GitHub issues. There is no formal roadmap process — priorities shift based on what's useful and what works.

## Becoming a maintainer

If you make sustained, high-quality contributions over time, maintainer access follows naturally. No formal process — just show up and do good work.

## Versioning

loom follows [Semantic Versioning](https://semver.org/). Until v1.0.0, breaking changes can happen in minor versions — pin your version if stability matters to you.
